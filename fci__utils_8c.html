unsigned char *  orbs

Calculate the parity and determinant resulting from a double excitation.

The parity is (-1) raised to the power of the number of occupied orbitals separating the occupied and virtual orbitals in the excitation

Parameters
[in,out]detPointer to origin determinant. Upon return, contains new determinant
[in]orbsThe indices of the two occupied orbitals (0th and 1st elements) and two virtual orbitals (2nd and 3rd elements) defining the excitation
Returns
parity of the excitation (+1 or -1)
